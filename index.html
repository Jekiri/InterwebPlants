How it works

Welcome to 🌱 InterwebPlants! Your goal is to keep your plant alive by watering it regularly. If you don't water the plant for a certain period, it will die. Here's how it works:

  • 🌧️ Click on the watering can to water the plant.
  • 🕒 If the plant is not watered within 12 hours, it will die.
  • 💧 Soil moisture levels will indicate the current state of the plant's water needs.
  • 🌳 Watch the plants grow over time as you keep them alive!
  • Note: This is a shared plant. You are watering the same plant with other people online.

Try to survive as many days as possible and see the plants reach their final form!
